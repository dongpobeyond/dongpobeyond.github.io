Chen Bo

CIP, ISCAS

Email: chenbo at iscas.ac.cn



I am currently an associate professor in Chinese Information Processing Lab. I obtained my PhD from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences under the supervision of professor Sun Le.

My thesis is here (Semantic Parsing based on Lexicon Learning and Structure Mapping).

My research interests include Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. Currently, I focus on Semantic Parsing, i.e., designing algorithms to map natural language sentence into formal meaning representation. I create a repository about semantic parsing on Github.
